About Us

We are an affordable Medical Centre that strives to do as much as we possibly can do help those who need our medical assistance. We have had many patients thank us for the service that we deliver and we plan to keep on doing good work.


Book Your Appointments Online

We now allow you to book your appointments online, which is much more convenient than physically booking an appointment, and the best part is... You get to choose the date, if there are no more spaces available on the chosen day, we will look for the closes available one, and contact you to confirm.


Make Payments Online

The same as with the appointments, the option to process a payment is now available online. All you need is your banking details and you are good to go. There is no complicated process attached to it, it is like simple online banking. Keep in mind that whether you have Medical Aid or not, the banking process should still be followed in case of any complications with the Medical Aid process.


Order Medicine Through Our Website

Order medicine online, the order will be sent to the sippliers and the medicine will be delivered to the centre. You will then have to come and pick it up, but there is no extra cost, and you get just what you ordered, so order medicine through us now!
